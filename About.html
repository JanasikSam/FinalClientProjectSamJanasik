About

Dr Baklava wants to share his extensive culinary knowledge with the world, with his home base being in his hometown of Queens, NY. With an extensive love for humans and their various cultures, Dr Baklava welcomes all to his shop for cheap yet delectable sandwiches inspired from his many travels.

Dr Baklava's Sandwich Shop is not just a tourist spot, nor is it exclusive to residents of the neighborhood. The shop welcomes all for a plethora of various culinary experiences, as well as fine art and things of that nature.
