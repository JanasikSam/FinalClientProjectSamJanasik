About The Doctor

Dr Baklava is a jack of all trades. Not only is he a seasoned culinary expert, he is also an avid painter, underground heavyweight wrestling champion, and father of 2.

The Doctor has traveled from coast to coast, continent to continent studying the greats to bring their sandwiches to you, (with his own spin on them of course.)

Baklava also happens to have a one sided beef with "Leathal Weapon 2" star, Mel Gibson. (Gibson gave The Doctor Covid at the Mayweather Vs Mcgregor fight)
